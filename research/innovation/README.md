# Innovation Documentation

This folder documents the innovative aspects of our research.

## Overview

This section articulates what makes our research novel, insightful, and ambitious compared to existing work.

## Innovation Categories

1. **Methodology Innovation**: Novel approaches or combinations of methods
2. **Application Innovation**: Applying methods to new contexts
3. **Integration Innovation**: Combining multiple approaches
4. **Insight Innovation**: New perspectives or findings

## Key Documents

- `novel_contributions.md` - What's new and unique
- `comparative_analysis.md` - How we differ from others
- `creative_approaches.md` - Innovative methods
- `impact_statement.md` - Potential impact

## Evaluation Criteria

For **Innovation in Hypotheses (20 marks)**, we demonstrate:
- ✅ **Novelty**: Original, not generic
- ✅ **Insight**: Deep understanding, new perspectives
- ✅ **Ambition**: Addressing important, complex problems
- ✅ **Impact**: Potential for significant contribution

## Innovation Checklist

- [ ] Clearly articulate what's novel
- [ ] Compare with existing approaches
- [ ] Demonstrate superior results or unique insights
- [ ] Show practical applicability
- [ ] Document creative approaches
- [ ] Quantify impact potential
