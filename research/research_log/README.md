# Research Log

This folder contains weekly research notes, insights, and findings.

## Purpose

The research log tracks:
- Weekly progress and findings
- Key insights discovered
- Challenges encountered and solutions
- Ideas and hypotheses that emerged
- Research decisions and rationale

## Weekly Notes

Each week, document:
1. **What was done**: Activities and tasks completed
2. **Key findings**: Important discoveries or insights
3. **Challenges**: Problems encountered and solutions
4. **Next steps**: Plans for the following week
5. **Ideas**: New ideas or hypotheses that emerged

## Structure

- `weekly_notes/week1_research.md` - Week 1 research notes
- `weekly_notes/week2_research.md` - Week 2 research notes
- `weekly_notes/week3_research.md` - Week 3 research notes
- `weekly_notes/week4_research.md` - Week 4 research notes
- `insights_and_findings.md` - Key insights and findings summary

## Contribution Tracking

Each team member should document their research contributions:
- Papers reviewed
- Hypotheses formed
- Insights discovered
- Challenges addressed

## Best Practices

1. **Regular Updates**: Update weekly notes each week
2. **Detailed Notes**: Document findings thoroughly
3. **Challenges**: Don't hide problems, document solutions
4. **Ideas**: Capture ideas even if not yet implemented
5. **Reflection**: Reflect on what worked and what didn't
